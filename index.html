J Cole Tribute Page

From Mix Tapes to Platinum Albums

Jermaine Lamar Cole is a Hip-Hop artist from Fayetteville, North Carolina who debuted with his mixtape The Come Up under the name J Cole in 2007 as a solo artist persuing a career. After signing to Jay-Z's Roc Nation in 2009 he released 2 more mixtapes before releasing his studio debut album Cole World: The Sideline Story which later became one of his 5 platinum albums.

No Features

In December 2014, J Cole released his first no feature album 2014 Forest Hills Drive. The album went on to become the first album to go platinum with no features in 25 years. As of now it has reached 3x Platinum with over 1.2 million album sales.

Consistent Platinum Albums

  1. Cole World: The Sideline Story: Platinum
  2. Born Sinner: Platinum
  3. 2014 Forest Hills Drive: 3x Platinum
  4. 4 Your Eyez Only: Platinum
  5. KOD: Platinum
